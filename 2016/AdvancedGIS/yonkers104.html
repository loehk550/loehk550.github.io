Yonkers




Greyston's goal for Open Hiring is to help alleviate poverty in Yonkers. The area of Southwest Yonkers has particularly low levels of educational attainment and high levels of poverty and unemployment. For these reasons, the Yonkers residents who will likely benefit most from Open Hiring are those living in the Southwest Yonkers area. The maps below show the discrepancies between the average household income, average housing prices, racial demographics, and population density of Southwest Yonkers and rest of Yonkers as a whole.
