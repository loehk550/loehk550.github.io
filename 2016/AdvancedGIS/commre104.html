Yonkers Community Resources





Greyston's PathMaking Services help unemployed individuals find services and help that put them on their Path to self-sufficiency, community contribution, and health. The resources in the map below serve as tools to help these individuals follow their own Path and can be further utilized by other businesses that utilize Greyston's Open Hiring model. Service categories can be chosen from the drop-down menu below the map.
