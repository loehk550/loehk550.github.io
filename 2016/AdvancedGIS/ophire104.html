Open Hiring Resources





Open Hiring is a hiring practice pioneered by Greyston Bakery and used by the business since 1982. Greyston hires all applicants who want to show up for and work hard, no questions asked. Greyston hires individuals without making them answer questions as to their work, educational, or crimal background. The Open Hiring model allows challenging-to-employ individuals to obtain self-sufficiency, with the ultimate goal of alleviating poverty in the Yonkers area. Greyston is seeking to expand their Open Hiring model to other businesses in Yonkers.
