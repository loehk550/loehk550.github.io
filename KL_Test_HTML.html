Assingment 5, Part 2: Brooklyn Middle Schools


This is a map of middle schools in Brooklyn, NY. If you click on each point on the map, the info window will display the name, principal and zipcode of the school. Please keep in mind that the principals are subject to change over time. There is also a link in each info window to a Google search of each school by name.

A link to the data is included below the map. Please note that this data is for all school in New York City and was edited down to only Brooklyn Middle Schools for the purposes of this map.
